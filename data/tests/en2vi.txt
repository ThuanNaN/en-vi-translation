Once a huge oak tree stood on the bank of a river. It was well nourished by the water of the river. Naturally, it was very strong and had a thick stem. Just nearby, grew some reeds with thin but flexible stems.They stood almost half in water and had flourished well too.
The Little Red Car: Once upon a time, there was a little red car named Ruby. Ruby lived in a small garage beside a quiet road. Every morning, she watched big trucks, buses, and motorcycles pass by. Ruby dreamed of going on a long journey. One sunny day, her owner, Jack, opened the garage door and said, “Today, we are going to the mountains.” Ruby was very excited. Her engine started with a happy sound: “Vroom!” They drove through the town, past green fields, and over a long bridge. The road became narrow and steep, but Ruby did not give up. She climbed the mountain slowly and carefully. At the top, Jack stopped the car and looked at the beautiful view. “You did a great job, Ruby,” he said. Ruby felt proud. She was not the biggest car or the fastest car, but she was brave and reliable. From that day on, Ruby knew something important: every journey is special when you keep moving forward.
